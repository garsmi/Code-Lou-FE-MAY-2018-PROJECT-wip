What Do I Do?

I am Garrett Smith, a graphic designer/ art dude out of Louisville, Kentucky. I am currently employed at Memetech, a screenprinting shop as the General Manager / Lead Designer. I freelance design and illustrations on the side and am currently enrolled in Code Louisville's Front End Web Development course.

I have worked with clients such as University of Louisville, University of Kentucky, Lydia House, Lou A Bull, Cumberland Brews, Home Skateshop and Lebowski Fest.

I enjoy working one on one with clients from consultation to final production . My clients get exactly what they need, when they need it. I dont mean to sound like a used cars salesman, I just want you to know that I take this serious. I love what I do, and I would love to work for you.
